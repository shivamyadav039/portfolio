Fake News Detection using NLP

Project Objective

To develop an intelligent system capable of automatically detecting fake news from textual content using deep learning techniques, specifically the BERT model, to combat the spread of misinformation.

Key Features & Implementation

  • Data Preprocessing: Merged and cleaned large datasets of real and fake news, preparing text for the transformer model.
  • Model Fine-Tuning: Utilized the pre-trained BERT model and fine-tuned it for binary text classification, achieving high accuracy.
  • Architecture: The solution uses BERT for sequence classification, leveraging its bidirectional understanding of language to capture nuanced patterns in text.
  • Evaluation: Achieved a validation accuracy of ~92%, demonstrating the model's effectiveness in distinguishing between legitimate and fabricated news.
